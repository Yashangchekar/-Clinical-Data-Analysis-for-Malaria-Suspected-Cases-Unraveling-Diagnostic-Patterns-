Description:
Explore a comprehensive analysis of clinical data from suspected malaria cases using various diagnostic parameters. The dataset includes information such as consent_given, location, Enrollment_Year, bednet usage, fever symptoms, and laboratory results including RDT, Blood culture, Urine culture, Taq_man_PCR, and Microscopy. The study aims to correlate these parameters with Clinical Diagnosis, providing valuable insights into the relationship between demographic factors, symptoms, and accurate diagnoses. This analysis sheds light on potential patterns, facilitating a deeper understanding of malaria diagnosis and aiding in the development of targeted treatment strategies.
